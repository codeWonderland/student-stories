Get Ready to Be Champlain Famous!

We created Tell Us About Yourself(ie) as a goofy, off -the-wall way to say, “Hey,” and introduce yourself to your soon-to-be classmates. All you need to do is answer at least 10 of our quirky fact questions—make sure you tell us at least one thing no one but you would know—on the form at champlain.edu/yourselfie. Then, upload a glamour shot, um, selfie, and you’re done. If we like your answers, we’ll highlight your profile and share your answers with your future Class (PS, there is almost no way we won’t love them—you’re family now!).
